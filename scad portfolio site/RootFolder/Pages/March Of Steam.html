March of Steam

The March of Steam boardgame was a project done in a group of three consisting of Robert Plante, Lauren Agne, and myself. It was heavily based on game Axis and Allies. My hand in the creation of the game was helping come up with Rules and concepts of the game and most of the cards that are part of the game.
